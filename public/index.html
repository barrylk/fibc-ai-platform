AI Capabilities

WHAT THE
AI DOES

📊 Daily Performance Analytics
Departments upload Excel reports each day. The AI instantly analyzes production vs targets, flags underperformance, and generates insights automatically.
🔮 Production Prediction Engine
AI forecasts next 30/60/90 days of production output based on historical trends — helping procurement plan material purchases before stockouts happen.
🧮 Material Requirements Planning
Based on production forecasts, the AI tells you exactly what to buy: yarn quantities, ink, polymer plates, lubricants, spare parts, roll stock — and when.
⚠️ Damage & Quality Intelligence
Track print damages, bag damages, weaving defects, and QC rejections. AI identifies patterns and root causes across departments over time.
💬 Ask Anything AI Chat
Natural language interface — ask "What was last week's printing efficiency?" or "How much yarn do we need next month?" The AI answers from your own data.
📈 Monthly & Yearly Reports
Automatic report generation for management — monthly production summaries, yearly trends, department comparisons, KPI scorecards, and financial efficiency metrics.

What was printing damage rate last week and how does it compare to our monthly average?
FIBC-AI
Based on uploaded printing reports, last week's damage rate was 2.4% (148 damaged bags out of 6,200 printed).

Your monthly average is 1.9%, making last week 26% above average. The spike appears on Wednesday and Thursday — correlating with the blue ink batch change. Recommend reviewing polymer plate alignment on Press #2.
You
How much ink stock do we need for next month's plan?
FIBC-AI
Based on 55,000 bags planned for next month and your current ink consumption rate of 18g/bag (4 colors), you'll need approximately 990 kg total ink. Current store stock shows 320 kg. Purchase order recommended: ~700 kg minimum with 10% buffer = 770 kg.
